Speak your first draft

Hi, I’m Speak Your First Draft
by Sasha Hedges

An app designed to get your first draft out of your head and onto the page.

This is Speak your first Draft. I designed this app to help writers plan, structure, and actually finish their stories. It combines outlining, character management, and flexible templates — without locking you into one way of writing.

This app gives you total freedom to write your first draft without distractions. Completely customizable, this app allows you to focus on your writing and not worry about formatting or structure.

Also, it's completely AI free. Write without any AI interference, theft, or bias.

Features

Speech-To-Text Edits

Speech to text can sometimes be horrific to use and distracting. However, it's incredibly helpful for getting your ideas down quickly. This helps bridge that gap.

Chapter Organization

Organize your chapters with prompts for writing an speech to text. Add summaries, scenes, notes, characters, and more.

Templates

Use pre-made templates to structure your writing and stay on track. Import custom templates to use for your personal projects.

Character Management

Manage your characters with detailed profiles, backstories, and relationships. Keep track of character development throughout your story.
